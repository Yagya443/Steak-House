About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by Chef Aryan Mehta, Steckhouse began as a small passion project built on a simple belief — great steak should feel like an experience, not just a meal.
What started as a single-grill kitchen has grown into a warm and cozy restaurant known for its rich flavors, premium ingredients, and genuine hospitality. With over three years of dedication, Chef Aryan and his team continue to craft dishes that honor authenticity, quality, and the love of good food

Our Culinary Philosophy

At Steakhouse, we believe great food begins with great ingredients. From hand-cut meats to fresh, locally sourced produce, every element on your plate is selected with intention. Our philosophy is simple — respect the ingredients, honor the process, and never compromise on quality. This approach has helped us create a menu that blends traditional craft with contemporary flavors.

The Steckhouse Experience

Dining with us is more than just enjoying a meal — it’s about feeling the warmth, comfort, and energy that make Steckhouse special. Our ambience, service, and flavors come together to create an experience that guests remember long after their visit. Whether you're celebrating a moment or simply craving good food, we aim to make every visit feel personal and memorable.

Craft Meets Creativity

Our team is driven by creativity, dedication, and a genuine love for cooking. From early morning prep to the final dish of the night, each member works with a shared goal: to deliver excellence. Their passion is reflected in every grill mark, every seasoning choice, and every plate that leaves the kitchen — making each dish a small work of art.
